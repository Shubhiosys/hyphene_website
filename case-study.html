The Challenge

Apex Health System faced a critical operational bottleneck: prior-authorization workflows. Highly specialized clinical teams were spending an average of 30+ hours weekly on manual entry—manually extracting data from charts, filling payer forms, and submitting requests.

This administrative burden led to significant clinician burnout, delayed patient treatment cycles, and a higher-than-average margin for transcription errors, which risked compliance integrity, billing accuracy, and increased claim denials.

The Solution

Hyphene deployed a custom prior-authorization layer tailored for the Apex Health hospital network. Utilizing our proprietary AI Orchestration layer integrated with their existing Epic EHR, the system automatically extracts clinical details from patient records to pre-fill payer forms.

Integration with existing EHR systems was completely seamless, requiring zero manual data porting and providing real-time operational validation that checks for payer rule compliance as the prior-auth requests are compiled.
