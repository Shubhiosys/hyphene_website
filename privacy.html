Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your healthcare data with the highest standards of security and privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our AI-powered healthcare operations platform:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Professional credentials and role information
  • Organization and facility details

Healthcare Data

  • Patient scheduling and staffing data
  • Operational metrics and analytics
  • Workflow automation data
  • Claims processing information

Technical Data

  • IP address and device information
  • Usage patterns and performance metrics
  • System logs and error reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI-powered healthcare operations platform
  • Process and analyze healthcare workflows
  • Generate operational insights and recommendations
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Improve our platform functionality and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with healthcare regulations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your healthcare data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions
  • HIPAA compliance with regular audits
  • SOC 2 Type II certification for security controls
  • Role-based access controls with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Comprehensive audit trails for all data access
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or healthcare data. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your consent - When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Service providers - With trusted third parties who help us operate our platform
  • Legal requirements - When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business transfers - In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

All third-party service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for authorized purposes.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability - Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Restriction - Limit how we use your information
  • Objection - Object to certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact our privacy team at privacy@hyphene.ai.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Fulfill legitimate business interests

Healthcare data retention periods are determined by applicable regulations and organizational policies.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session and preferences
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Personalize your experience
  • Remember your login information

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some platform features.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy determinations for approved countries
  • Binding corporate rules for intra-organizational transfers

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 18 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.

Significant changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notifications
  • Platform announcements
  • Website notifications

Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
