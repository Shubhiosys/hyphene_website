Company Culture

Values that Drive Us Forward

Our team thrives on a set of core principles that help us collaborate, innovate, and make a real impact on healthcare operations.

🎯

Impact-First Mindset

Every line of code we write and every decision we make aims to improve the lives of nurses, doctors, administrators, and patients.

⚡

Deep Technical Innovation

We tackle hard problems at the intersection of AI/ML, clinical workflows, and complex integrations, pushing boundaries daily.

🤝

Empathy & Respect

We listen actively, foster psychological safety, and build an environment where everyone can speak up, take risks, and be themselves.

🔑

Extreme Ownership

We trust our teammates completely. We define outcomes, own the execution, and celebrate both successes and learnings together.

📚

Continuous Learning

Technology and healthcare evolve fast. We provide ample learning budgets, promote knowledge sharing, and support career growth.

✨

Pragmatic Execution

We value getting things done. We iterate fast, gather feedback, and continuously polish our software until it shines.

Hiring Journey

Our Simple Interview Process

We value your time. We aim to move candidates from first call to final offer within two to three weeks.

1

Application

Submit your resume. We review every profile carefully within 3 business days.

2

Intro Call

A 30-minute chat with our recruiter to align on background, values, and role expectations.

3

Role Fit

A deep dive technical assessment or design review simulating real-world work tasks.

4

Panel Review

A structured discussion with cross-functional team members to talk culture and fit.

5

Offer

We finalize competitive compensation, make a formal offer, and prepare to welcome you!
